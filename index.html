        Author Of The Week

        Emily Henry

        Style: Contemporary Romance & Women’s Fiction

        Emily Henry is a New York Times bestselling author acclaimed for her emotionally resonant romantic comedies. Her books—including Beach Read, People We Meet on Vacation, Book Lovers, and Happy Place—combine clever wit, emotional depth, and introspective storytelling. Her work explores the complexities of modern love, grief, and personal growth, earning her both critical praise and a loyal fanbase. Emily’s ability to write tender yet hilarious fiction has redefined what readers expect from the romance genre.
